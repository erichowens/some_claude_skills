2. **First paragraph includes keyword**

   - Every article links to skill detail pages

4. **Build backlinks**
   - Submit to llms.txt (search engine optimization for LLMs)
   - Guest articles on dev.to, Hashnode
   - Contribute to awesome-lists

5. **Consistency**
   - Publish 2+ articles/month (signals active site)
