## PART 3: YOUTUBE SHORTS / TikTok TEMPLATES

### How to Extract Shorts From a Skill Spotlight Video

**Length:** 15-45 seconds (YouTube optimal: 30-45 sec, TikTok: 15-60 sec)

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-3 sec)** - Catchy statement or visual
2. **Demo (4-15 sec)** - Show the skill in action
3. **Result (16-25 sec)** - Show the outcome
4. **CTA (26-30 sec)** - Where to get it

**Extraction Tips:**
- Use CapCut (free, available on Mac) to edit
- Add captions automatically (speech-to-text)
- Speed up boring parts (CapCut "time remap" feature)
- Add skill name graphic at 0:05 and 0:25
- Add CTA text at end: "someclaudeskills.com/skills/[name]"

---

## Notes & Philosophy

**Why consistency matters:**
- Algorithms favor regular posting (YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn)
- Audiences expect predictable cadence (news feed habits)
- Batching prevents burnout (do it once, post all week)
- Quality > Quantity (but consistency beats sporadic perfection)

**Why the Win31 aesthetic matters:**
- Every post should be visually distinctive
- Use consistent branding (colors, fonts, graphics)
- This builds recognition (people remember you)
- It's your unfair advantage in a crowded space

**Why community-first approach works:**
- People follow people, not brands
- Discord > social media for retention (owned audience)
- User-generated content is your best marketing
- Celebrating wins builds loyalty and word-of-mouth
