### When You Want to Quit

```
Before quitting, ask:

1. Is this a bad idea or am I just tired?
   (If tired: Take a week off. Revisit after.)

2. Have I given it a fair shot?
   (Fair shot = 3 months of consistent effort)

3. Is there any traction?
   (Even 5 happy users = signal)

Sunk cost fallacy is real.
But make sure you're quitting the right thing
for the right reasons.
```
