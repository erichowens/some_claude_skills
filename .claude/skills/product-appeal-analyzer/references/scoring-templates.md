## Priority Formula

Calculate recommendation priority:

```
Priority = (Users Affected Ã— Severity) / Fix Difficulty

Where:
- Users Affected: 1-100 (percentage)
- Severity: 1-10 (impact on conversion)
- Fix Difficulty: 1-10 (effort required, lower is easier)

- Confusing headline: (100% Ã— 9) / 3 = 300 â†’ HIGH PRIORITY
- Missing testimonials: (80% Ã— 6) / 5 = 96 â†’ MEDIUM PRIORITY
```
