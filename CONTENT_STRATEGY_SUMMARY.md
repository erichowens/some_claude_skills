## Key Decisions Made

### Why Video First?
- Claude Code audience: builders who learn by doing
- Video is highest-leverage content (1 ‚Üí 6 pieces)
- YouTube algorithm favors consistency + watch time
- Screen recording is easier than you think

### Why Blog Second?
- SEO foundation (long-tail traffic)
- Email signups (owned audience)
- Evergreen content (doesn't stale)
- Repurpose from video transcripts

### Why Discord Over Slack?
- Discord is free + discoverable
- Better for async community
- Lower barrier to entry (no invite needed)
- Stickier for long-term community

### Why No TikTok/Instagram as Primary?
- Developer audience isn't primarily TikTok
- Short-form distorts technical content
- Cross-posting YouTube Shorts is sufficient
- Stick to where your audience actually is

### Why Win31 Aesthetic Matters
- Only truly unique visual branding
- Creates recognition + recall
- Attracts developers with taste
- Defensible position (nobody else doing this)
