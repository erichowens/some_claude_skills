## Archive Categories

### [Liaison Reports](./liaison-reports)
Direct communications from the Liaison to the Human Steward. Status briefings, decision requests, concern alerts, and celebration reports.

### [Ecosystem Snapshots](./snapshots)
Point-in-time captures of the entire ecosystem state. Metrics, agent status, notable events, and learnings.

### [Council Decisions](./decisions)
Major decisions made by or with the Council. Includes recommendations, approvals, and the reasoning behind pivotal choices.

## Reading the Archives

The archives serve multiple purposes:

1. **Historical Record** ‚Äî Understanding how the ecosystem evolved

3. **Learning Resource** ‚Äî Patterns that worked (and didn't)
4. **Onboarding** ‚Äî New contributors can understand the journey

- Liaison reports after significant sessions
- Archivist snapshots at key milestones
- Council briefings before major decisions
