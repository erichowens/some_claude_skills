# SEO Visibility Expert

Get your web projects discovered by both traditional search engines AND AI systems. This skill covers the full spectrum from technical SEO to social launch strategies.

## Quick Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Category** | Marketing & Growth |
| **Activation Keywords** | SEO, discoverability, llms.txt, robots.txt, Product Hunt, launch strategy |
| **Tools Required** | Read, Write, Edit, Bash, WebFetch, WebSearch |

## What It Does

The SEO Visibility Expert skill helps you:

- **Technical SEO audits** - Identify and fix SEO issues
- **llms.txt setup** - Optimize for AI crawlers (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity)
- **robots.txt configuration** - Proper permissions for modern bots
- **Schema.org/JSON-LD** - Rich snippets and structured data
- **Launch strategies** - Product Hunt, Hacker News, Reddit playbooks
- **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** - Get cited by AI systems
- **Core Web Vitals** - Performance optimization

## Key Capabilities

### AI Crawler Optimization

Create `/llms.txt` at your site root with structured information about your project. AI systems use this to understand and reference your content accurately.

### Product Hunt Launch Strategy

- Launch at **12:01 AM PST exactly**
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Build hunter network 2-4 weeks before
- Spread engagement throughout the day

### Hacker News Strategy

- Show HN format for new projects
- Technical deep-dives, not marketing language
- Post between 6-8 AM PST for US audience
- Be genuine, humble, and responsive

## When to Use

**Use for:**
- Technical SEO audits and fixes
- Setting up llms.txt for AI crawlers
- Configuring robots.txt for modern era
- Schema.org/JSON-LD structured data
- Launch strategies (Product Hunt, HN, Reddit)
- Core Web Vitals optimization

**NOT for:**
- Paid advertising/PPC campaigns
- Social media content creation
- Email marketing campaigns
- Brand strategy/naming

## Quick Wins Checklist

1. ✅ Create llms.txt at site root
2. ✅ Add JSON-LD schema to key pages
3. ✅ Verify robots.txt allows AI crawlers
4. ✅ Generate sitemap.xml
5. ✅ Check Core Web Vitals score above 90
6. ✅ Add Open Graph tags for social previews
7. ✅ Create humans.txt
