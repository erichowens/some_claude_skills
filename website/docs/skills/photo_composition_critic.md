## Core Competencies

### Visual Weight & Balance (Arnheim)

Beyond rule of thirds - understanding what makes compositions work:

- **Size:** Larger elements carry more visual weight
- **Color:** Warm/saturated colors are heavier than cool/desaturated
- **Isolation:** Isolated elements demand attention
- **Position:** Upper-right carries more weight (Western reading pattern)
- **Intrinsic Interest:** Faces, text, unusual shapes attract the eye

### Computational Aesthetics

Leverage ML models trained on aesthetic datasets:

- **AVA Dataset:** 250K+ images with mean scores from 78-549 votes
- **NIMA (Google):** Predicts score distributions, not just means
- **LAION-Aesthetics:** Filtered dataset used to train Stable Diffusion
- **VisualQuality-R1:** 2024 reasoning-augmented quality assessment
